Our project workflow ensures that you receive fully tested and documented code on schedule and on budget. Here's how it works.

If you like what you see, click on the lightbulb and fill out the idea form with your name, email, and idea so we can help you with your new project!

 

Step One

You have a great idea. You present it to us. We help you transform your idea into stories from the user's perspective. You prioritize those stories and select the top priority stories for the first sprint.

 

Step Two

We write tests for the user stories selected for the sprint. We write fully documented code until the tests pass and the stories are satisfied. We update an online burndown chart daily to show you our progress.

 

Step Three

When all stories for the sprint pass, we present them to you. You approve each story. We get paid for the complete sprint. We work with you to desgin the next sprint.
