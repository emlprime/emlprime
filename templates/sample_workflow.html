Here is a detailed breakdown of our scrum project workflow. If you have a great idea, click 'You have a great idea. You present it to us.' to go back to the form on the work page. From there, you can submit your idea to us, and we can help you make it happen.

You have a great idea. You present it to us.
We help you transform your idea into stories from the user's perspective. This is the backlog.
We help you prioritize the stories and select the first ones for the initial sprint.
You select the tasks for the sprint. We assign point values.
We write tests to verify that new code is needed for the stories.
We write fully documented code until the tests pass and the stories are satisfied.
We update the burndown chart daily to show you our progress.
When all stories for the sprint pass, we present them to you.
You approve each story.
We get paid for the complete sprint.
We work with you to desgin the next sprint.
