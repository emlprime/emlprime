Our rates can be broken down into three categories: start-up costs, maintenance costs, and coding costs.

We provide all the services necessary for you to launch a new web project. We offer domain registration, and e-commerce establishment in addition to our programming services. Domain registration is a flat $50 fee, and the e-commerce link to an existing merchant account is $300.

We also provide full hosting and maintenance services. Hosting, e-commerce maintenace, https certification, and periodic backups are among the many services we provide for your website. A full monthly or quarterly cost estimate for your site is available upon request.

Our programming services are available at the cost of $100 per task point. We assign point values to each task in your project backlog and charge per point - thus ensuring that the project will be completed at the quoted price.

Here's an example of what an invoice might look like:

  • Domain Registration: $50
  • E-Commerce Set-Up: $300
  • 10 points @$100/point: $1000
  • Total: $1350

We ask for a 25% deposit before we begin work on a sprint. The remaining 75% of payment is due when the project has been completed and meets all your expectations.
